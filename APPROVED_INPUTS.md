## 🔑 Key Success Factors

### MUST HAVE for Approval:
1. **Credit Score: 750+** (Excellent tier)
2. **DTI Ratio: <40%** (Total EMIs < 40% of monthly income)
3. **LTI Ratio: <3.0** (Loan < 3× annual income)
4. **Employment: 3+ years** in current job
5. **Low Debt: <₹10K** existing EMIs

## 📊 Quick DTI Calculator

**Formula:**
```
Monthly Income = Annual Income ÷ 12
Proposed EMI = Loan Amount ÷ Tenure (approx)
Total EMIs = Existing EMIs + Proposed EMI
DTI Ratio = (Total EMIs ÷ Monthly Income) × 100
```

**Example:**
```
Income: ₹8,00,000/year = ₹66,667/month
Existing EMIs: ₹5,000/month
Loan: ₹15,00,000 for 20 years
Proposed EMI: ~₹8,333/month
Total EMIs: ₹13,333/month
DTI: 13,333 ÷ 66,667 × 100 = 20% ✅ (Good!)
```
